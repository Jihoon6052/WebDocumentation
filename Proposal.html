Background

Of the countless transactions available in the Internet space, the website that I will design and create is the ticket exchange website. Many ticket-trading websites can be found on the Internet now, such as bizzabo, seatedly, etc.

In the past, ticketing, which was only available on-site, has become available on the Internet since the early 2000s. Since then, online ticket issuance has been increasing over the years.

Research

Before, I talk about ticketing website, it is really important to talk about 'Elements that can make a business website successful'. According to Allison Bowlus, few factors makes small business website successfully.

Which is;

  • Obtain a good domain name
  • Make home page (landing page) a to-the-point summary
  • Keep forms simple
  • Make it easy for visitors to contact you
  • Regularly create and publish quality content


After I found out the factors, I realized that when creating a website, I should make it as simple and easy to use as possible.

Ideation

As I mentioned on research, the website should be easy and simple to use. It would be good for users to use the website that only has simple necessary information, rather than having useless information.

Because everything people ask for is different, website needs to be able to meet as many things as possible, and because there are many users who like cheap prices, website will put a subscription function within the website itself to carry out events or ticket promotions.


Overall, the elements needed for the website

  • Easy and simple to use
  • Subscription function
  • Sell various tickets, such as concert ticket, meuseam tickets, etc.

Designs

Depending on how I make the design of website, the number of users can increase or decrease. Therefore, I think it is most important to use a design that is as conspicuous and convincing as possible. An easy and simple-to-use design will be the best design.

Wireframes

Wireframes of landing page, sign up page and end page.


Basically, Brand logo is located on top left corner. Sign up and Log in button located on top right corner. On the middle of homepage, there are breif information about ticketing website and subsribe membership.
